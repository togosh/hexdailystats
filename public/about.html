HEX Daily Stats was created to keep historical records of the HEX ecosystem in daily snapshots throughout time. These records can be used for research, educational and analytical purposes.


Ryan and Togosh have been in HEX since the beginning and are big time HEX enthusiasts. They met at the 1st HEX Vegas Meetup and decided to team up for the project.
